\section{Objective}
Identify, assess, and mitigate technical and project risks associated with developing and using AymaraLang.

\section{Methodology}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Risk identification.
    \item Probability and impact assessment.
    \item Mitigation and contingency plans.
\end{itemize}
