\section{Objective}
Define how AymaraLang and its compiler will be verified and validated to ensure compliance with requirements.

\section{Scope}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \texttt{aymc} compiler (lexer, parser, semantic, codegen).
    \item Experimental LLVM backend.
    \item Minimal runtime.
\end{itemize}

\section{V\&V strategy}
\begin{longtable}{@{}p{3cm}p{10cm}@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Type} & \textbf{Description} \\ \midrule
Inspection & Code and documentation review \\
Test & Execution of automated test cases \\
Analysis & Comparison against expected results \\ \bottomrule
\end{longtable}

\section{Verification matrix (summary)}
\begin{longtable}{@{}p{3cm}p{3cm}p{7cm}@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Requirement} & \textbf{Method} & \textbf{Evidence} \\ \midrule
FR-01 & Test & Compiles multiple \texttt{.aym} files \\
FR-05 & Test & Binary runs examples \\
FR-07 & Test & \texttt{.ll} generation \\
NFR-03 & Inspection & Readable error messages \\ \bottomrule
\end{longtable}

\section{Acceptance criteria}
\begin{itemize}
    \item All examples in \texttt{samples/} must compile and run.
    \item \texttt{make test} must pass without errors (when the environment allows).
\end{itemize}

\section{Evidence artifacts}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Execution reports from \texttt{make test}.
    \item Build logs and example output.
\end{itemize}
